Chapter II.

Mr Tulliver, Of Dorlcote Mill, Declares His Resolution About Tom

“What I want, you know,” said Mr Tulliver,—“what I want is to give Tom a good eddication; an eddication as’ll be a bread to him. That was what I was thinking of when I gave notice for him to leave the academy at Lady-day. I mean to put him to a downright good school at Midsummer.
